The University Residential Center is located at Via Aldruda Frangipane, 6 in Bertinoro (Telephone: +39 0543 446500 Fax: +39 0543 446599). The workshop takes place in the Bertinoro castle, the reception is located a bit down the road at Via Aldruda Frangipane, 4 in Bertinoro.

Bertinoro lies approximately half-way between Bologna and the Adriatic coast town of Rimini. If you are arriving by car or taxi, you can obtain driving instructions from most starting points using Google Maps.

By train

For those attending Eurocrypt 2024 in Zurich, there are convenient train connections to Forlì (1-change) from Zurich main station, with high-speed trains running multiple times daily. Proceed as indicated below from Forlì station.

By plane

For those flying in, the nearest major airport is the Bologna Guglielmo Marconi International Airport (BLQ) which has good connectivity to many major European locations. You may also want to check for flights to the Rimini (RMI) Federico Fellini Airport, reachable from several European cities via low cost companies.

Another possibility, especially for those arriving from North America, is to arrive at one of the two Milan airports (which have direct flights from a number North American cities) then proceed to the Milan Central Train Station, take a train to Forlì, and from there continue by taxi to Bertinoro. Train schedules can be found here.

If you arrive at Rimini Airport, you should take a train (direction Bologna or Milan), get off at Cesena (about a 20-minute trip), and then take a taxi to Bertinoro.

If you arrive at Forlì airport, probably the easiest option for reaching Bertinoro is to hire a taxi. If you arrive at Bologna airport or train station, proceed as indicated below.

Bologna Airport or Train Station to Bertinoro by Taxi

This is going to cost you about 140 EUR, including highway tolls, but it becomes an affordable option if you share it with a group of 3-4 people. For more detailed information, consult or call CoTaBo Taxi.

Bologna Airport to Bologna Center and Train Station

Guglielmo Marconi International Airport, which serves Bologna, is about 10 km to the north-west of the city center. The most convenient option for getting to Bologna train station is to take the Marconi Express monorail line (12.80 EUR).

Bologna Train Station to Forlì Train Station

Numerous trains run between Bologna and Forlì. The trip takes 30-45 minutes depending on the train type. Please make sure that you get off at Forlì station and not Forlimpopoli (which is indeed closer to Bertinoro but has no bus service for continuing your trip). For complete train departure times and further information, please consult the Italian Railways web site.

Forlì Train Station to Bertinoro

The simplest way to reach Bertinoro from Forlì is to take a taxi. The trip should cost around 20 EUR, and you may ride-share with 3-4 people.

Spending the Night in Bologna

If you are lucky, you can find a room in the very nice, wonderfully located and inexpensive University Guest House. We highly recommend it. The only problem is finding a room. Email bookings are possible through the above site. Otherwise you have to resort to a hotel.
